# Financial Analysis – The Coca-Cola Company

## Executive Summary

This project evaluates whether The Coca-Cola Company represents a strong investment opportunity for Software Solutions based on its financial performance between 2020 and 2023.

Using the CORE framework (Context, Overview, Ratios, Evaluation), the analysis assesses Coca-Cola’s profitability, liquidity, efficiency, and capital structure. The company demonstrated strong revenue growth and improving capital efficiency, as reflected in rising Return on Capital Employed (ROCE). However, operating margins declined slightly due to increasing costs, highlighting emerging pressure on profitability.

Overall, Coca-Cola maintains a strong financial position supported by stable liquidity and consistent performance. The findings suggest that the company remains a viable long-term investment, provided it continues to manage costs effectively and sustain operational efficiency.

## Business Problem

Should Software Solutions invest in The Coca-Cola Company based on its financial performance and long-term growth potential between 2020 and 2023?

## Methodology

* Applied the CORE analysis framework (Context, Overview, Ratios, Evaluation)
* Analysed financial statements: income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow
* Evaluated key financial ratios, including:

  * Return on Capital Employed (ROCE)
  * Operating profit margin
  * Current ratio
  * Gearing ratio
* Used Microsoft Excel to organise, calculate, and visualise financial data
* Considered external factors such as inflation and post-pandemic economic recovery

## Key Insights

* Revenue increased significantly over the 4-year period, indicating strong business growth
* ROCE improved, reflecting more efficient use of capital
* Operating margins declined slightly due to rising operating costs
* Liquidity remained stable, indicating strong short-term financial health
* The company maintained a balanced capital structure, supporting financial stability

## Business Investment Recommendations
Based on the analysis, Coca-Cola demonstrates strong financial performance and stability, making it a suitable investment option. 

* **Other Business Recommendations**
* **Strengthen Cost Management to Improve Margins**
  Rising costs are impacting profitability. Coca-Cola should focus on cost optimisation across production and supply chain operations.

* **Leverage Strong ROCE for Strategic Investments**
  Efficient capital use provides an opportunity to invest in innovation, digital transformation, and emerging markets.

* **Enhance Operational Efficiency**
  Implementing automation and data-driven decision-making can improve productivity and reduce inefficiencies.

* **Maintain a Balanced Capital Structure**
  While liquidity is strong, maintaining an optimal balance between debt and equity will minimise financial risk.

* **Expand Product Diversification and Market Reach**
  Continued expansion into healthier product lines and international markets will support long-term growth.

## Conclusion

Based on the analysis, Coca-Cola demonstrates strong financial performance, stability, and long-term growth potential. Despite some margin pressure, the company’s overall financial health supports a positive investment decision.
