# Financial Analysis – The Coca-Cola Company

## Executive Summary
This project presents a financial analysis of The Coca-Cola Company using the CORE framework (Context, Overview, Ratios, Evaluation) as part of my MBA.

The analysis evaluated the company’s financial performance over four years (2020–2023), focusing on profitability, liquidity, efficiency, and capital structure. The findings supported a positive investment outlook based on strong revenue growth and stable financial performance.

## Business Problem
How can financial statement analysis be used to evaluate a company’s performance and inform investment decisions?

## Methodology
- Analysed financial statements (income statement, balance sheet, cash flow)
- Used Microsoft Excel to organise and evaluate data
- Calculated key financial ratios including:
  - Return on Capital Employed (ROCE)
  - Operating profit margin
  - Current ratio
  - Gearing ratio
- Considered external factors such as inflation and post-pandemic recovery

## Key Insights
- Revenue increased significantly over the 4-year period, indicating strong growth  
- ROCE improved, showing efficient use of capital  
- Operating margins declined slightly due to rising costs  
- Liquidity remained stable, indicating good short-term financial health  

## Business Recommendation
Based on the analysis, Coca-Cola demonstrates strong financial performance and stability, making it a suitable investment option. Continued focus on cost management could further improve profitability.
