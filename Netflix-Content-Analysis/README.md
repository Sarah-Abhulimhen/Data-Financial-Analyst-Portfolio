# Netflix Content Analysis (Excel + Power BI)

## Overview
This project presents an end-to-end data analysis of Netflix content, focusing on uncovering patterns in content growth, genre popularity, ratings distribution, and duration trends.

The analysis combines **Excel (data cleaning & exploratory analysis)** with **Power BI (interactive dashboard)** to transform raw data into actionable insights.

## Business Problem
Streaming platforms rely on data to guide content investment decisions. This project explores Netflix’s dataset to answer key business questions about:

* Content growth trends
* Viewer classification (ratings)
* Content duration patterns
* Genre performance
* Geographic production distribution

## Key Questions

* How has Netflix content evolved over time?
* What is the distribution between movies and TV shows?
* Which genres dominate the platform?
* What ratings are most common?
* What is the typical duration of content?
* Which countries produce the most content?

## Data Cleaning (Excel – Power Query)

* Removed duplicates
* Replaced missing values with **"Unknown"**
* Standardized formats
* Extracted numerical values from duration
* Created new feature: **Duration Type (Movie vs TV Show Seasons)**

## Analysis (Excel)

Built pivot tables and charts to analyze:

* Top 10 genres
* Ratings distribution
* Movie duration distribution
* TV show season distribution
* Top producing countries

Developed an Excel dashboard to summarize findings.

## Dashboard (Power BI)

Created an interactive dashboard featuring:

* Content growth over time
* Distribution of content by rating
* Top genres on Netflix
* Global content distribution map
* KPI cards (Total Titles, Movies, TV Shows)
* Year slicer for dynamic filtering

## Key Insights

* Netflix content experienced rapid growth after **2016**, peaking around **2019–2020**
* Movies dominate the platform (~70%), significantly more than TV shows
* Most movies fall within the **90–120 minute range**, indicating standard viewing preferences
* **TV-MA** is the most common rating, suggesting a focus on mature audiences
* The **United States** is the leading content producer
* **Drama** and **International Movies** are the most popular genres

---

## Recommendations

* Continue investing in movie content due to higher volume and demand
* Expand high-performing genres such as Drama and International Movies
* Diversify ratings to attract wider audience segments
* Increase production in emerging global markets
* Optimize content duration based on viewer preferences
